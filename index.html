The Experience

A Curated Culinary Sanctuary

Tucked away in the historic village of Ella, Old Goa, Ana is an intimate boutique restaurant designed for the true culinary explorer. Seating only 25 guests, Chef Vasquito blends local heritage flavors with timeless Portuguese culinary methods, crafting an unforgettable gastronomic journey.

Every dish tells a story of cultural convergence—using locally sourced coastal ingredients, house-spiced pork, fresh morning catches, and carefully guarded family recipes, served in a cozy, simple setting that prioritizes connection, flavor, and absolute warmth.
