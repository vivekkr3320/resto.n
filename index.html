Old Goa Heritage

Fresh, Neat & Authentically Prepared

Located directly in the historic core of Old Goa, Goan Classic Family Restaurant and Bar offers a culinary rest stop for travelers and locals alike. Perfectly situated near the landmark Se Cathedral, we serve a fresh, neat, and highly rated selection of coastal Goan fish plates, local curries, and sizzling kababs.

Guests can choose between our cool **Air-Conditioned family dining room** or our pleasant **open-air sheltered veranda** surrounded by green coconut palms. We provide ample parking at the backside (accommodating up to 10 vehicles) and clean modern restrooms to ensure your comfort.
